A Brief History

Puneites reference‘Dorabjee’s’ fondly as “Apru Dorabjee’s”… and this is and has been its unique selling point. Since 1911, this centurion is still run by four generations of the family with the same passion and gusto!

Since its acquisition from the British in 1911, by Dorabjee Patell, Dorabjee's still runs today with the same passion and gusto, by the same family. Over a century old, Dorabjee’s is possibly

India’s oldest supermarket and referred to by its customers and patrons as an institution of trust and integrity.

In keeping with its USP - It’s indeed a better way to shop! Dorabjee’s from its inception has catered to discerning customers who cherish a delightful shopping experience, exemplary service and an array of the choicest of products. Pre Independence, Dorabjee's built up a reputation as an importer and re-seller of quality goods from the world over. Since the anchor store was and is still located in the Civil Cantonment area of Poona it was patronised

by the officers of the British Army and British ‘sahebs’, of the Civil Service. After Independence, although most of the imported products, ceased to be available, Dorabjee’s continued to provide prime quality local wares to its shoppers along with the same superior service that its known for. With the advent of liberalisation, and the opening of imports, Dorabjee's, once again is famed as a top retailer for merchandise both Iocal and from across the globe. Today Dorabjee’s has six stores in Pune, situate across prime locations of Camp, Viman Nagar - near the airport, NIBM/ Kondwa, at the Pavillion Mall - Senapati Bapat Road, Balewadi High Street at Balewadi and at Amanora Mall Annex Hadapsar having a combined shopping area of 95,000 square feet.

Here you find the widest of ranges across categories like a delicatessen with a variety of cold cuts, antipasti and cheeses; shop in shops for a fine selection of local and foreign wines and beers; confectionery, candy and chocolates, cosmetics and personal care products; the choicest in health and beauty; pet care; baby foods and products; organic and health food options galore; the pick of the freshest produce; international and Indian beverages and foods - which include a wide array of sodas, juices, malt drinks, teas and coffees, staples, groceries, oils, grains, noodles and pasta, breakfast foods, breads, cookies and crackers, snacks, sauces and dressings, ready to eat and boxed meals, pickles and preserves, baking ingredients, tinned and canned foods, condiments… to name but just a few; an extra large space offering a vast variety of dairy, ice cream, frozen and chilled non vegetarian and vegetarian foods; a fresh seafood, poultry and meats section; small home appliances; bake-ware, glassware, crockery and cutlery; products galore for the home, table and kitchen. Relax over a cuppa or sample our scrumptious menu at great prices, at the in-house bakery counters.

The family like always, continues to stay in touch with its customers, always interested in what they have to say and at the same time continuing to provide them with new and select merchandise and a first-class shopping experience. All in All a visit to Dorabjee’s, is a must whether you're native to Pune or just visiting; with its cheerful ambiance, choice merchandise mix, the personal touch et all … Satisfaction Guaranteed!!
